This section should define the criteria that will be used to evaluate model performance. For classification tasks, common metrics include accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score, and ROC--AUC. You may also justify why certain metrics are more appropriate than others, especially when the dataset is imbalanced.

For example, you may discuss the following:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Accuracy for overall correctness,
    \item Precision for minimizing false positives,
    \item Recall for minimizing false negatives,
    \item F1-score for balancing precision and recall, and
    \item ROC--AUC for evaluating separability across thresholds.
\end{itemize}
